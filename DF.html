Designated Fighters

Designated Fighter represents the best of the best within Army Special Forces, composed of the most skilled and battle-hardened operators across all units. This premier force is divided into two highly specialized teams: Noble and Omega. Noble Team excels in First-Person Shooters, participating in Scrimmages (Tournament Grounds) and Raids (Raid Grounds), where the precision, speed, and combative abilities of all operatives are crucial in high pressure and intensity engagements. Omega Team leads the forefront in Wargames, showcasing superior tactical awareness, coordination, and strategic dominance. As Army Special Force's most formidable fighting force, Designated Fighter sets the standards for combative excellence.

NOBLE TEAM COMMAND

Noble Team stands as the elite of the elite within the ASF, surpassing even Alpha Company in skill, precision, and combat effectiveness. Specializing in First-Person Shooter operations, Noble Team dominates the battlefield through unmatched reflexes, tactical mastery, and seamless coordination. The team is divided into two top-tier detachments: The Big Dawgs (operating in the Eastern Standard Time zone) and Asian Squad (operating in the Australian Eastern Standard Time zone). Together, they set the gold standard for FPS excellence across all ASF units.

OMEGA TEAM COMMAND

Omega Team is the ASF’s undisputed master of wargames, recognized as the best of the best in large-scale simulated combat operations. With unparalleled strategic vision, flawless coordination, and exceptional adaptability, Omega Team dominates every scenario they enter. Operating without detachments, this elite unit functions as a single, highly cohesive force, ensuring maximum efficiency and precision in every mission. Omega Team embodies the pinnacle of ASF’s war gaming capability.
