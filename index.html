The United States Army Special Forces, also known as the "Green Berets", is an elite Tier 2 Special Operations unit that specializes in unconventional warfare and counterinsurgency. As the go-to force of the Army Special Operations Command, they are tasked with missions involving local influence, insurgency development, or neutralizing threats in civilian environments, operating with top precision and adaptability. The Green Berets are also well known for their expertise on the field, cohesion among one another, as well as their exceptional ability to operate to accomplish both quiet and lethal effectiveness. Always focused and relentless, the Green Berets will never falter until the mission objective has been completed, earning them the title of being the most formidable force within the United States Army and one to be reckoned with.

What does Army Special Forces offer?

Army Special Forces will provide you an introduction to various special operations skills and talents, as well as numerous benefits. Some of the skills you can learn as a member of Army Special Forces include parachuting, breaching, land navigation, and more, with countless opportunities to apply these skills through scrimmages, raids, wargames, operations, and weekly deployments. By joining Army Special Forces, you can also expect a fair rank progression system that will promote members based off of seniority and activity, a realistic and decently low weekly quota, the ability to earn numerous badges, and much more. As well, Army Special Forces has several Military Occupation Specialities that candidates can aim for, allowing members to vary from a Direct Action Specialist in the 1st Special Forces Group, to a Medical Sergeant in the 5th Special Forces Group, and even as an Cold Weather Leaders Course Instructor in the 10th Special Forces Group; all the while being a part of an amazing community. Additionally, members of Army Special Forces are authorized to wear Tigerstripes on base, a Green Beret that corresponds with their Special Forces Group, and even Army Special Operations Command Blackouts.

Elite Groups within Army Special Forces

1st Special Forces Group

The 1st Special Forces Group serves as the primary combative force of Army Special Forces, structured into two specialized companies: Alpha and Bravo. Alpha Company is the more elite company, tasked with operations centered towards First Person Shooters, participating in Scrimmages (Tournament Grounds) and Raids (Raid Grounds), where the precision, speed, and combative abilities of all operatives are crucial. Bravo Company on the other hand, also handles First Person Shooters, aiding and supporting Alpha Company. To learn more about the 1st Special Forces Group, click the corresponding button below.

5th Special Forces Group

The 5th Special Forces Group serves as the highly specialized unit within Army Special Forces, structured into two elite companies: Echo and Delta. Echo Company is renown for its specialization in Wargames, demonstrating exceptional strategic, tactical, and operational effectiveness in large-scale combat scenarios. Delta Company is dedicated to Military Simulation (MILSIM), focusing on realism, breaching, and immersive tactical operations which mirror real-world military special operations. 10th Special Forces Group

The 10th Special Forces Group serves as the dedicated training and recruitment unit within Army Special Forces, while maintaining its capabilities of deploying in active operations. The 10th Special Forces is structred into two specialized companies, Whiskey and Yankee. Whiskey Company specializes in First-Person Shooters and plays a critical role in mentoring, training, and developing the combative effectiveness of the 1st Special Forces Group. Yankee Company is focused on Wargames and Military Simulation (MILSIM), playing a critical role in mentoring, training, and developing the combative effectiveness of the 5th Special Forces Group. Designated Fighters

Designated Fighter represents the best of the best within Army Special Forces, composed of the most skilled and battle-hardened operators across all units. This premier force is divided into two highly specialized teams: Noble and Omega. Noble Team excels in First-Person Shooters, participating in Scrimmages (Tournament Grounds) and Raids (Raid Grounds), where the precision, speed, and combative abilities of all operatives are crucial in high pressure and intensity engagements. Omega Team leads the forefront in Wargames, showcasing superior tactical awareness, coordination, and strategic dominance. As Army Special Force's most formidable fighting force, Designated Fighter sets the standards for combative excellence.
