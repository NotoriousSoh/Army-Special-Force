5th Special Forces Group

The 5th Special Forces Group serves as the highly specialized unit within Army Special Forces, structured into two elite companies: Echo and Delta. Echo Company is renown for its specialization in Wargames, demonstrating exceptional strategic, tactical, and operational effectiveness in large-scale combat scenarios. Delta Company is dedicated to Military Simulation (MILSIM), focusing on realism, breaching, and immersive tactical operations which mirror real-world military special operations. DELTA COMPANY HIGH COMMAND

Delta Company is Army Special Force's premier Military Simulation (MILSIM) unit, specializing in realistic, highly disciplined operations that replicate real-world military tactics, procedures, and mission profiles. The company is organized into two elite detachments: Operational Detachment Alpha (ODA) 5141 and Operational Detachment Alpha (ODA) 5142. Both detachments operate with precision, and professionalism, conducting complex simulated operations with tactical expertise. Delta Company stands as the company representing MILSIM within Army Special Forces.

ECHO COMPANY HIGH COMMAND

Echo Company is the Army Special Force's elite wargames unit, renowned for its mastery of large-scale simulated combat and strategic operations. The company is composed of two highly skilled detachments: Operational Detachment Alpha (ODA) 5151 and Operational Detachment Alpha (ODA) 5152. These detachments excel in executing complex battle plans, coordinating multi-unit manoeuvres, and adapting to dynamic combat scenarios with precision and efficiency. Through relentless training and tactical innovation, Echo Company maintains its position as the dominant force in Army Special Force's wargames operations.
